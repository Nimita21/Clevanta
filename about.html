Our Story

Clevanta is a family-founded consulting firm built on experience, trust, and thoughtful leadership.

Founded by Rajeev Priyadarshi and co-led with his daughter Nimita Priyadarshi, Clevanta blends senior enterprise strategy and architecture expertise with modern UX/UI and design thinking. What began as a shared vision between generations has grown into a consulting practice focused on clarity, calm leadership, and relationship-driven growth.

Rajeev brings decades of experience in enterprise systems, digital transformation, and strategic IT leadership. Nimita brings a human-centered design perspective, ensuring technology solutions are intuitive, accessible, and aligned with real user needs.

Together, they created Clevanta to help organizations move forward with confidence—where systems are not just built to scale, but designed to serve.
